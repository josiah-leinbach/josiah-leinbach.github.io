Anglican Studies

Traditional BCP Parishes

I keep a registry of all traditional Book of Common Prayer (BCP) parishes in the Anglican Church in North America (ACNA), the Anglican Church of Australia (ACA), and the Church of England; both to better understand the variegated notion of what constitutes a "traditional prayerbook", but also to assist people find parishes that use a specific traditional prayerbook.

The registry is crowdsourced and users may submit additional parishes that are not listed on the map.
